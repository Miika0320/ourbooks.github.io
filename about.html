About Our Books

A personal book rental and reading space designed for book lovers.

Our Story

Our Books was founded to provide a peaceful reading space where book lovers can come together, rent books, and enjoy a quiet reading environment. Inspired by the aesthetics of Airbnb, we designed a platform where users can search for books, book private reading rooms, and even enjoy a curated menu of snacks and beverages.

Our Mission

We aim to create a comfortable and modern reading experience where readers can access a curated library, book a personal reading space, and enjoy refreshments. Our platform is designed for book lovers of all ages and backgrounds.
